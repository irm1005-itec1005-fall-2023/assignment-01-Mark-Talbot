About the team

General Information

  • The team was founded in 1992
  • Main front office members are:
    • General Manager Pierre Dorion
    • Owner Michael Andlauer
    • Head Coach DJ Smith
  • Have qualified for the playoffs in 16/30 seasons
    • Most recently in 2017 where they reached the Eastern Conference Finals
  • Finished 21st out of 32 teams in the 2022/23 season
